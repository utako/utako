About Me



I'm a recovering physicist based in San Francisco, CA.

Back in 1993, my brother introduced me to telnet chat servers. Soon after, I started making my own basic HTML websites dedicated to the sorts of things 13-year-old girls like (Smashing Pumpkins, pictures of horses and MS Paint drawings). Since then, my specialty has turned towards web design using Ruby on Rails, Javascript and responsive HTML and CSS.

My personal interests include hiking, riding bicycles, playing music, painting, and drinking bourbon.
